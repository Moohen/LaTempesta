Barok w Radości Music Festival, founded by Jakub Burzyński and Julita Mirosławska, takes place each year since 2012 in the southern realm of Warsaw, organized by Musica Humana Association. The venue presents masterpieces of early music repertoire, from chamber works to oratorio or opera scores, performed by outstanding artist from Poland and abroad. Special guests appeared on the festival were, among others, Jeremy West, Marco Vitale, Jan Ciřmař, Michaela Koudelková, Robert Bachara, Wojciech Parchem, Andrzej Zawisza, Matej Benda, Pandolfis Consort and Sabionetta. Special events – like Johannes-Passion in 2015 or B Minor Mass in 2017 – are presented in addition to the regular festival formula . LA TEMPESTA is a host ensemble of the festival since its beginning. Artistic Director of Barok w Radości is Jakub Burzyński.
